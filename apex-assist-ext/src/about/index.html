About Apex Assist

Apex Assist is a Chrome extension designed to automate quizzes and tests on Apex Learning using AI technology.

How it Works

The extension analyzes questions and answer options on screen, optionally captures a screenshot for image questions, calls your configured AI provider, and selects answers automatically.

Privacy & Security

  • API keys are stored in Chrome storage on your device.
  • When enabled, screenshots of the visible tab are sent to your AI provider.

Use responsibly and in accordance with your institution's policies.
