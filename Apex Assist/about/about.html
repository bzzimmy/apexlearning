About Apex Assist

What is Apex Assist?

Apex Assist is a Chrome extension designed to automate quizzes and tests on Apex Learning using AI technology.

The extension uses Google Gemini AI to analyze questions and provide accurate answers, making your learning experience smoother and more efficient.

How it Works

Apex Assist analyzes the questions on your screen, processes them using advanced AI, and automatically selects the most likely correct answer. The extension supports both text-based questions and questions with images.

  • Automatically reads questions and answer choices from the Apex Learning interface
  • Processes images when necessary for context-rich questions
  • Uses Google's Gemini AI to determine the correct answer
  • Automatically selects the answer and moves to the next question
  • Configurable delay between answers to mimic natural timing

The extension can be configured to deliberately miss some questions to make your results appear more natural and avoid detection.

Privacy & Security

Apex Assist respects your privacy. Your API key and all question data are processed locally in your browser.

When you use Apex Assist:

  • No user data or questions are stored on external servers
  • Your questions are sent directly to Google's Gemini API using your own API key
  • All processing happens in your browser
  • Your information stays on your device
  • We don't collect any analytics or usage data
